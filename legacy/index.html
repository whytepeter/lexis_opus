How to read fiction

Fiction teaches rhythm, tone, dialogue, and emotion. Don't worry about every word — read for the story first. Log words that feel important. Start with short stories before tackling novels.

Vocabulary

How the study mode works

Choose how many words to study. Each card shows a word — try to recall the meaning, then tap to flip and see the definition and an example sentence. Rate yourself: Got it, Fuzzy, or Blank. Blank words repeat in the same session. Fuzzy words are shown more next time. Get a word right 3 sessions in a row and it's Mastered.
